## Information

Splash page is an opening before entering your links. Splash page can improve profesionalism and branding presence to your audience. Make sure to follow these steps:

![Splash](/splash.jpeg)

## Steps

<Steps>
<Step>

### Log in to your Account at IsmyBio

Click here to [Login](https://ismy.bio/login)

</Step>
<Step>

### Navigate to the Splash Page

Click on the Splash Page menu on the left side of the dashboard.

</Step>
<Step>

### Create Splash Page

Click on the `Splash Page` button to start.

</Step>
<Step>

### Adjusting & Customize

Adjust and Customize the Splash Page according to your needs.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Splash Screen Properties

You can adjust these Attributes and Properties to apply to your Splash Screen:

- Logo
- Favicon (Web Icon)
- OpenGraph Image
- Countdown Timer (Before showing content)
- Auto Redirect
- Secondary Button Name & URL
- Advertisment (Header & Footer)
- Custom CSS & JS
