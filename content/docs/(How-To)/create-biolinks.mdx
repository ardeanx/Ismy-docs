## Information

In this page, you will learn how to create and modify your BioLinks.

## Steps to Create

<Steps>
<Step>
 
### Login to your Account at IsmyBio

Click here to [Login](https://ismy.bio/login)
 
</Step>
 
<Step>
 
### Navigate to Biolink Page

Click on the Biolink menu on the left side of the dashboard.
 
</Step>

<Step>

### Press Add New Button

Click on the Add New button to create a new biolink.

</Step>

</Steps>
