## Information

Biolinks are a great way to share your contact information with others. They are a digital business card that can be shared via email, text message, or social media. Biolinks can include your name, phone number, email address, website, and more. You can create a Biolink using a variety of tools, including online VCard generators, email programs, and contact management software. In this article, we'll show you how to create a Biolink using the Contacts app on your iPhone or iPad. In this page you will learn how to create and modify your biolinks.
