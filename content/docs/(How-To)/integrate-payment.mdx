## Information

Yes.. At IsmyBio you can Integrate your own Payment Gateway so user can pay/purchase directly your Product, Service, and give donation to you in your IsmyBio Links. Make sure to follow these steps:

![Payment](/payments.jpeg)

## Steps

<Steps>

<Step>

### Log in to your Account at IsmyBio

Click here to [Login](https://ismy.bio/login)

</Step>

<Step>

### Navigate to the Payment Processor Page

Click on the Payment Processor menu on the left side of the dashboard.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add Payment Processor

Click on the `Add Payment Processor` button to start.

</Step>

<Step>

### Select Payment Processor

Select the type of Payment Processor you want to Integrate

</Step>

</Steps>

## Options Available

Currently we only support these Gateway:

- PayPal
- Stripe
- Crypto.com
- Mollie
- Paystack
- RazorPay
- Midtrans
