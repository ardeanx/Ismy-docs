## Information

The steps to shorten long links are so simple. To start shorten a link, you need to follow the steps below:

![Gif](/shortlinks.gif)

## Steps

<Steps>

<Step>

### Log in to your Account at IsmyBio

Click here to [Login](https://ismy.bio/login)

</Step>

<Step>

### Navigate to the Shorten Link Page

Click on the Shorten Link menu on the left side of the dashboard.

</Step>

<Step>

### Paste the Long Link

Paste the long link you want to shorten in the input field.

</Step>

</Steps>

After you shorten the link, you can view and manage all the links you have shortened in the Shorten Link page. 

<Callout type="info">
You can also customize the shortened link by clicking on the `Created Link`.
</Callout>
