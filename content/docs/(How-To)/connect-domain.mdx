## Information

Unlock full potential of complete rebranding by Connecting your own domain with IsmyBio. We offer this feature 100% free and you can start using it directly in IsmyBio. Make sure to follow these steps to connect your own domain:

![CustomDomain](/custom-domain.jpeg)

## Steps

<Steps>
<Step>

### Log in to your Account at IsmyBio

Click here to [Login](https://ismy.bio/login)

</Step>
<Step>

### Navigate to the Custom Domain Page

Click on the Custom Domain menu on the left side of the dashboard.

</Step>
<Step>

### Connect Domain

Click on the `Connect Domain` button to start.

</Step>
<Step>

### Fill Information

Type your Domain or Subdomain name for the rest input field you can adjust it according to your prefences.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Note

<Callout type="info">

Your domain or subdomain must have an A record pointing to 152.53.93.249 or CNAME record pointing to ismy.bio.

</Callout>
