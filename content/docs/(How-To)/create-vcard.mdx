## Information

VCards are a great way to share your contact information with others. They are a digital business card that can be shared via email, text message, or social media. VCards can include your name, phone number, email address, website, and more. You can create a VCard using a variety of tools, including online VCard generators, email programs, and contact management software. In this article, we'll show you how to create a VCard using the Contacts app on your iPhone or iPad.
